Track your expenses

Do you find it hard to track your expenses, how much was spent on particular commodities,(food/eat out, transport, subscription, groceries)etc? worry no more. With track it, you can do that in real time.

How do i use track it?

Link your bank account

  • Connect your bank account you make transfer with
  • Once that is done, track it automatically generates the amount spent and designate it to the nearest commodity
  • View the total in real time

Input the figure manually

  • Any amount spent on commodity, go to track it and input it in the designated category
  • Once that is done, view your total in real time
